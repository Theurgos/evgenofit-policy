# EvgenoFit

EvgenoFit is an iOS app for fitness logging and education. Log sessions, track sets and personal records, browse a curated exercise catalogue.

## What it is and is not

EvgenoFit is for fitness logging and education. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Consult a qualified professional for health concerns.
