# EvgenoFit Support

Need help with EvgenoFit? This page covers the most common questions.

## Contact

Email: evgenofit@gmail.com.

Replies usually come within a few days. Please include your iPhone model, iOS version, and a description of what you expected vs what happened.

## Common questions

### How do I sign in?

EvgenoFit uses Sign in with Apple as its only sign-in method. On the welcome screen, tap **Sign in with Apple** and follow the system prompts.

### How do I sign out?

Open the Dashboard, tap the gear icon in the top right, then tap **Sign out** under the Account section.

### How do I delete my account and all my data?

Open the Dashboard, tap the gear icon in the top right, scroll to the Account section, tap **Delete account**, and confirm. You will be asked to sign in with Apple again to confirm it is you, then your account, sessions, sets, and Apple authentication entry are removed permanently. This cannot be undone.

### How do I switch between kilograms and pounds?

Settings → Units → Weight. Your historical data stays accurate. Only the display changes.

### Why does the Active session screen ask me to confirm before discarding?

To protect data you've already logged. Discarding deletes the in-progress session and all sets you logged inside it. Finishing a session saves it normally.

### My personal record card is empty.

Personal records appear once you log at least one set for that exercise. The four flagship lifts shown on Dashboard are Barbell Bench Press, Lat Pulldown, Leg Press, and Lateral Raise.

### Is EvgenoFit medical advice?

No. EvgenoFit is for fitness logging and education only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Consult a qualified professional for health concerns.
